## Executive Status

- The simulator, paper feature stack, Attn-LOB pretraining, PPO, DQN, paper baselines, real-data loader, synthetic validation, diagnostics, and Euler submission pipeline are implemented on this branch.

- Synthetic data quality can be made high by the current quality gate; the latest `flowvol` generator variant scored `99.63`, but author-reward C-PPO still overtraded and lost heavily.

- Pure-PnL PPO can be profitable on synthetic data and some stress symbols, but AS often remains stronger.

- Paper-faithful author reward is the main blocker: PPO and DQN learn tighter, high-turnover behavior that improves shaped reward while hurting PnL.

- DQN mechanics can learn profitable low-inventory policies when explicit inventory-risk reward is added, but that is non-paper-faithful.

- Real AAPL/GOOGL support works technically, but `REAL_EVENT_STRIDE=100` creates high-trade-density, directional 2000-event windows; current real PPO evals are negative and should be treated as smoke tests, not final evidence.
